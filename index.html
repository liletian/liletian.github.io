About Me

I'm a hardware engineer specializing in high-speed SerDes (Serializer/Deserializer) architecture and mixed-signal IC design. I focus on developing transceivers that enable the next generation of computing and networking.

With expertise in analog design, signal integrity, and high-frequency layout, I tackle complex challenges like equalization, clock data recovery (CDR), and low-power circuit topologies at advanced technology nodes.
